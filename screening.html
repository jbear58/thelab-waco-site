Membership

Member safety screening.

The Lab is open 24/7 and our members often train alone. Every membership includes a one-time safety screening — the same check, for everyone, no exceptions.

Applied uniformly to every applicant

Why we screen

A keyed 24/7 facility runs on trust. Limited availability keeps the floor uncrowded; screening keeps it safe. Members should feel comfortable training at 2 PM or 2 AM.

What we check

  • Public sex-offender registries (national and Texas)
  • Criminal conviction records, using the name and date of birth on your application (verified against your photo ID at key pickup)

What declines an application

  • A conviction for sexual assault, or any offense involving a minor
  • Current registration on any sex-offender registry
  • A theft-related conviction (theft, burglary, robbery) within the past 7 years

Nothing else in your history is considered, and results are kept confidential.

How it works

  • Sign up online — you'll provide consent as part of your application
  • Screening is completed within 2 business days
  • Once cleared, your 24/7 access is activated when you pick up your key and show ID
  • If an application is declined, monthly dues are refunded in full (the one-time signup fee covers the screening)
  • Think a record isn't yours or isn't accurate? Contact us — we'll re-screen at no cost
